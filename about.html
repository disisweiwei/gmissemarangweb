Gandhi Memorial Intercontinental School

"Where Every Child is a Promise"

We at Gandhi Memorial International School are committed to providing high quality education and lifelong learning opportunities to the Community. To realize this aim, our endeavor has been to strike a balance between modern infrastructure and an internationally acceptable education system.

English is the principal medium of instruction and Bahasa Indonesia, Mandarin , Hindi are part of the language curriculum.

The school follows the Indian and U.K. curricula and pattern of education.

University of Cambridge takes care of Primary and Lower Secondary Curriculum. The school offers International GCSE and AS/A Levels, recognized by Oxford AQA.

The Matriculation examination is conducted at the end of Grade 10 and the Higher Secondary at the end of Grade 12. The Higher Secondary Certificate awarded to successful candidates of this examination is accorded recognition on an all India basis as equivalent to Indian Higher Secondary Certificate (10+2 scheme). The Matriculation and Higher Secondary Examinations are conducted by Board of Secondary Education Indian Schools, Indonesia which is recognized by the Association of Indian Universities of India, U.S.A., U.K., Australia , Singapore , Malaysia etc.

From Principal's Desk...

WDear Parents,

We , here at GMIS , firmly believe in the African proverb that says, "It takes the entire village to educate and train a child." Education is a shared responsibility involving the home, community, and school, and fostering a positive relationship among these stakeholders is crucial for a child's success. In our rapidly evolving, technology-driven world, it is through our collective efforts and commitment to excellence that we prepare our children for a dynamic and unpredictable future.

Our school's mission goes beyond academic achievement. We aim to nurture well-rounded, compassionate global citizens who appreciate and respect diverse cultures.

The greatest gift we can give humanity is EDUCATION. Education is not just about helping our children acquire knowledge from books and other sources, but about awakening their intellect to use that knowledge effectively. We believe that the journey of learning is boundless and should extend far beyond the confines of classrooms and textbooks. Our goal is to instill a lifelong love for learning in our children, turning every moment into an opportunity for growth. At Gandhi Memorial Intercontinental School Semarang, we celebrate the joy of learning.

We maintain a holistic focus on our children's physical, intellectual, and emotional well-being, dedicating ourselves to their overall development.

In August 2018, our school moved to a new campus amidst the environmentally friendly, lush green slopes of Candigolf Hills. To provide a world-class international education, we have affiliations with prestigious institutions such as Oxford/AQA for International GCSE, AS/A Levels, and Cambridge International for Primary and Lower Secondary.

If you have any suggestions for further improvement or concerns about any aspect of our school’s functioning, please do not hesitate to contact me. I assure you that your suggestions will be valued.

Best regards,
Suresh Arora.
